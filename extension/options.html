🧲 PropMail Pinpoint

Finds the full address of a Rightmove listing by reverse-geocoding its map pin and matching it against the official EPC (Energy Performance Certificate) register. You provide your own free EPC API key — it stays on your device.

Saved ✓

The key is stored locally with chrome.storage.local and is sent only to the official EPC register API over HTTPS. It is never shared with anyone else.

How to get a free EPC API key

  1. Go to get-energy-performance-data.communities.gov.uk and register (it's free, run by the UK government).
  2. Confirm your email and sign in to get your API key / token.
  3. Paste it above and click Save.

The EPC register covers England & Wales. Each property with an Energy Performance Certificate has a publicly available full address.

How it works

  1. Pinpoint magnet — reads the map pin (lat/long) Rightmove embeds in the page and reverse-geocodes it to the nearby real postcodes via postcodes.io.
  2. EPC database — searches those postcodes in the EPC register for every certified address on the street.
  3. Match — narrows by property type (flat vs house) and floor area to rank the most likely full address.

Use the results responsibly and in line with Rightmove's terms and applicable data-protection rules.
