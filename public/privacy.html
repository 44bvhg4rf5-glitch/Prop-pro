Privacy Policy

How we handle personal data, in line with the UK GDPR and the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR).
This notice explains what we collect, why, and the rights you have. If you have questions, or want to object to or stop any processing, contact us using the details at the end.

Who we are

PropMail Pro is an estate and letting agency operating across the Harrow (HA) postcode area. We are the data controller for the personal data described in this policy.

The data we collect

  • Valuation & enquiry details you give us — your name, contact details, property address and any message.
  • Property & owner information from public records — for example HM Land Registry, Companies House, and local-authority planning registers — used to identify the likely owner of an on-market property for postal contact.
  • Technical data — basic, privacy-respecting analytics needed to run the website securely.

How and why we use it (lawful basis)

  • To respond to your valuation/enquiry — on the basis of your request and our legitimate interest in providing the service.
  • Postal marketing to property owners — under our legitimate interests, balanced against your rights. We screen against the Mailing Preference Service (MPS) and our internal do-not-mail list, and we stop on request.
  • We do not make unsolicited marketing calls, texts or emails to individuals without the consent required by PECR.

Sharing & retention

We do not sell your data. We share it only with service providers who help us operate (e.g. secure hosting), under appropriate safeguards. We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose, then delete or anonymise it.

Your rights

  • Access a copy of your data, or have it corrected or erased.
  • Object to direct marketing at any time — we will stop and add you to our do-not-mail list.
  • Restrict or object to other processing, and request data portability where applicable.
  • Complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Contact

To exercise any right, object to marketing, or ask a question, please contact us. (Full registered company details, ICO registration and redress-scheme membership are shown in our website footer.)
