# 🏢 Board report report — 2026-06-29

## The week in one line
This week, the team focused on optimizing prospecting letters, monitoring competitor activity, reviewing live tools for improvements, and conducting a security audit to ensure the platform's overall performance and competitiveness.

## What the team found & did
* **Research**: The UK residential property market is experiencing steady growth, with increasing buyer confidence and a stabilizing market. Free tools are available for address discovery, marketing, and client engagement, which could impact PropMail Pro's competitive edge. The team recommends integrating these free tools and tailoring services to meet the needs of the target market.
* **Competitor**: Spectre, the main competitor, is continuously enhancing its all-in-one marketing platform, introducing new features such as AI-powered predictive models, automated prospecting, and marketing tools. PropMail Pro is behind Spectre in terms of the scope of its marketing platform and the use of AI-powered predictive models.
* **R&D**: The current tools in place include a live property finder, address resolution engine, and AI Intel/owner research. However, the tool is hitting limits in terms of accuracy and deliverability, particularly with flat-level letterbox data. The team recommends exploring new data sources and integrating free tools to enhance the platform's services.
* **Ops/Health**: The live site's performance is healthy, with all components functioning correctly. However, the OS Places data source is capped due to a free-trial limit, and the team is awaiting an upgrade to OS Premium.
* **Security**: A security audit of the backend code is underway to identify and mitigate potential security risks.

## Recommended decisions for the owner
1. **Invest in AI-powered predictive models**: To stay competitive, PropMail Pro should consider investing in AI-powered predictive models to enhance its marketing platform.
2. **Integrate free tools and data sources**: The team recommends integrating free tools and data sources to enhance the platform's services and provide more value to users.
3. **Upgrade to OS Premium**: To overcome the limitations of the free-trial cap, the team recommends upgrading to OS Premium to access more comprehensive data.
4. **Prioritize user experience and training**: The platform should focus on enhancing user experience, providing training and resources on best practices, and facilitating communication between agents and clients.
